heroes

In Dota 2 there are 4 types of attributes- Strength, Agility, Intelligence, and Universal. Every hero has all the attributes, however one of the attributes is the hero's main attributes. Therefore, heroes are divided into 4 groups according to their main attribute.

  • strength

    Strength heroes in Dota 2 are durable, tanky heroes with high health pools and the ability to absorb damage. They often serve as frontline fighters, initiating battles and protecting teammates. Many have crowd control abilities, making them key to disrupting enemies and sustaining in long fights.

  • agility

    Agility heroes in Dota 2 are fast, flexible, and excel at dealing damage. They typically have high attack speed and rely on their mobility to outmaneuver opponents. Agility heroes often scale well into the late game, becoming powerful damage dealers, and are key in both physical damage and carry roles. Their abilities often focus on enhancing their attack power or evading damage, making them essential for fast-paced combat and carrying teams to victory.

  • intelligence

    Intelligence heroes in Dota 2 are powerful spellcasters with high mana pools. They excel in support, crowd control, and dealing damage with abilities. While more fragile, their impact lies in their strategic spells that control fights and support their team.

  • universal

    Universal heroes in Dota 2 have a balanced combination of attributes, making them versatile in various roles. They can adapt to different situations, with abilities that scale well with both strength, agility, and intelligence. These heroes offer flexibility in gameplay, allowing players to build them in a variety of ways depending on the needs of the team. Their versatility makes them effective in a wide range of scenarios.
