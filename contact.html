DOta 2 tournaments

The International (TI) is the premier Dota 2 tournament, hosted annually by Valve. It features the world's best teams competing for the Aegis of the Immortal and a multi-million dollar prize pool, often boosted by community contributions. Since its first event in 2011, TI has grown into one of the biggest esports tournaments, known for its intense competition, high-level gameplay, and thrilling moments. The tournament represents the pinnacle of Dota 2's competitive scene.
