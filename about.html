About

defence of the ancients

Dota 2 is a free-to-play multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game where two teams of five players compete to destroy the enemy's Ancient. Players control unique heroes with different abilities, requiring strategy, teamwork, and skill. With over 120 heroes and a thriving esports scene, including the prestigious The International, Dota 2 offers endless depth and competitive excitement.

Gameplay

In Dota 2, two teams of five players control unique heroes, each with special abilities. The objective is to destroy the enemy's Ancient while protecting your own. Players earn gold and experience by defeating enemies and creeps, leveling up and buying items to strengthen their heroes. Success relies on teamwork, strategy, and tactical battles as players control lanes, secure objectives, and outmaneuver their opponents.

A map full of adventures

The Dota 2 map consists of two sides with three main lanes (Top, Middle, Bottom) and a jungle in between. Players battle through the lanes, fighting for control and pushing towards the enemy base. How to download

To install Dota 2, first download and install the Steam client from steampowered.com if you don't have it yet. Create a free Steam account or log in if you already have one. Once logged in, search for Dota 2 in the Steam store, and click "Play Game" to start the free download and installation. After the game is installed, you can launch it directly from your Steam library and begin your journey into the world of Dota 2.
